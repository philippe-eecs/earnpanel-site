A tiny panel on your Mac that pays you while you work.

EarnPanel sits always-on-top in a corner of your screen. When a paid opportunity matches your profile — a survey today; sponsored watch-and-answer videos and offers are rolling out — it appears in the panel, you complete it, and your balance gets a share of what the advertiser or network pays us. Keep the panel bigger, keep a bigger share.

Requires macOS 13 or later. US residents, 18+ only.

Pick your panel size — it sets your share

Every survey has a real dollar payout from the network. Your panel size decides how much of that raw payout is yours:

  • Mini 55% 300 × 140 pt — stays out of your way
  • Standard 62% 340 × 460 pt — more offers visible
  • Expanded 70% 400 × 640 pt — the most offers, the best share

A bigger panel shows more offers and earns a higher share of each payout. You can switch sizes anytime; the share applied to a survey is the tier your panel was in when you completed it.

What you can earn from

  • Surveys — live. Market-research studies matched to your profile, most paying $0.50–$1.50 per completion.
  • Watch & answer — rolling out. Short sponsored videos with a quick comprehension question at the end. You're paid for verified attention, not for leaving a video running — the reward unlocks only after the video finishes and you answer correctly.
  • Offers & tasks — coming. Try an app, complete a signup, scan a receipt. Payouts vary by task.

Everything appears in the same panel and credits the same balance. We never sell "passive ad watching" — advertisers pay for proven engagement, and that's what we share with you.

How it works

  1. Install EarnPanel and sign in. Fill out a short demographic profile so survey networks can match you to studies you actually qualify for.
  2. Leave the panel open while you work. Surveys appear when there's a match. Most take 5–15 minutes.
  3. Complete surveys, earn credits. Each completion credits your balance with your tier's share of the network's raw payout, in US dollars.
  4. Wait out the 45-day hold. Survey networks audit completions and reverse low-quality ones. Credits clear after 45 days.
  5. Cash out from $5. Once your cleared balance reaches $5, redeem via gift cards or PayPal.

Honest FAQ

How much will I actually earn?

Not much — and we'd rather tell you that up front. Most surveys pay roughly $0.50 to $1.50 each, and how many you qualify for depends on your demographics and the day. Think of EarnPanel as coffee money that accumulates passively while you work, not a side income and definitely not a wage. If anyone promises you real income from surveys, be skeptical.

Why the 45-day hold before I can cash out?

Survey networks review completions for quality and can reverse ones they judge fraudulent, rushed, or inconsistent — sometimes weeks after the fact. The 45-day hold means we only pay out earnings the networks have actually stood behind. If a completion is reversed after you've already been paid for it, the reversal nets against your future earnings, which means your balance can temporarily go negative.

Why does a bigger panel pay more?

Screen space is the honest currency here. A larger panel surfaces more offers and signals you're more engaged, so we pass along a larger cut: 55% at mini, 62% at standard, 70% at expanded. There's no trick — the percentages apply to the same raw network payout either way.

What happens to my data?

To match you to surveys, we share the profile information you give us with survey partners like BitLabs, and we're paid when you complete their surveys. Under California law that sharing may count as a "sale" of personal information, so we give you a clear way to opt out. Read the privacy policy for the full picture before signing up.

Why do I get screened out of surveys?

Researchers need specific demographics, and networks often can't tell whether you qualify until you've answered a few questions. Screen-outs are frustrating and mostly unpaid; they're a fact of the survey industry, not something we can eliminate.

Do I owe taxes on this?

Possibly. Rewards are promotional payments, not wages. If your payouts approach IRS reporting thresholds in a calendar year, we'll ask you to submit a W-9 before further payouts and may issue a 1099-MISC. See the terms.

Who can use EarnPanel?

US residents who are 18 or older, on a Mac running macOS 13 or later. One account per person and per device — multi-accounting forfeits balances.

Does my balance ever expire?

If your account is inactive for 12 months, small remaining balances may expire under our dormancy policy. Cash out when you hit $5 and this never matters.
