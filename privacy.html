EarnPanel ("we," "us") is a macOS application that pays you to complete surveys supplied by third-party survey networks. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. EarnPanel is available only to United States residents who are at least 18 years old.

1. What we collect

CategoryExamplesSource
Account information Email address, account identifier You, at sign-up
Profile demographics Age range, gender, ZIP code, household, employment, and other demographic answers you choose to provide for survey matching You, in the profile questionnaire
Device information A device identifier for your Mac, macOS version, app version Collected automatically by the app
Panel usage Panel size tier, periodic heartbeats confirming the panel is open, session timing Collected automatically by the app
Survey activity Survey completion, screen-out, and reversal events reported by survey partners; associated payout amounts Survey partners (e.g., BitLabs)
Payout information Payout method details (e.g., PayPal email), redemption history; taxpayer information (W-9) if your earnings approach IRS reporting thresholds You, at cash-out

We do not collect the contents of your screen, your keystrokes, your browsing history, or the substance of your survey answers (those go directly to the survey partner running the survey).

2. How we use it

  • To match you to surveys you may qualify for;
  • To credit your earnings correctly, including applying the payout share for your panel size tier;
  • To detect and prevent fraud, multi-accounting, and abuse;
  • To process payouts and comply with tax reporting obligations;
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service and provide support.

3. Who we share it with — and why this may be a "sale"

We share your profile demographics, device identifier, and account identifier with survey partners (currently BitLabs, and potentially other networks in the future) so they can match you to surveys. We are paid by those partners when you complete their surveys. Because we receive payment in connection with this sharing, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) it may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not claim otherwise, and you can opt out (see Section 4).

We also share information with:

  • Service providers that host our infrastructure, authentication, and payout processing, under contracts limiting their use of your data;
  • Payout providers (e.g., PayPal, gift card issuers) as needed to deliver your redemption;
  • Tax authorities where reporting is legally required;
  • Law enforcement or others when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service;
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

California residents (and residents of other states with similar rights) may opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information. To opt out:

  • In-app: open EarnPanel Settings and turn on "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"; or
  • By email: send a request to privacy@earnpanel.app with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share."

Global Privacy Control: we honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals as a valid opt-out of sale/sharing for the browser or device that transmits them.

Please note: opting out stops us from sharing your profile data with survey partners, which means we can no longer match you to surveys. You may keep your account and any existing balance, but new survey offers will not be available while the opt-out is in effect.

5. Your California privacy rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, sell, or share, and to request a copy;
  • Delete personal information we hold about you, subject to legal exceptions (e.g., records we must keep for tax or fraud-prevention purposes);
  • Correct inaccurate personal information;
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (Section 4);
  • Non-discrimination: we will not deny you the service, charge you different prices, or provide a different level of quality because you exercised these rights. (As noted above, opting out of sharing makes survey matching impossible as a functional matter — that is a consequence of how survey matching works, not a penalty.)

To exercise these rights, use the in-app privacy controls or email privacy@earnpanel.app. We will verify your identity via your account email before acting on a request. You may use an authorized agent with written permission.

6. Texas residents (TDPSA)

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act, we will not process sensitive data (such as data revealing racial or ethnic origin, religious beliefs, health conditions, sexual orientation, or precise geolocation) without your consent. Some survey-matching profile questions are optional and may touch on these categories; we ask for your consent before collecting them, and you may decline — declining only reduces the number of surveys you match. Texas residents also have rights of access, correction, deletion, portability, and opt-out of sale/targeted advertising, exercisable through the same channels as Section 5, including an appeal process if we decline a request.

7. Data retention

  • Account and profile data: kept while your account is active and deleted or de-identified within 90 days of verified account deletion, except as noted below.
  • Earnings ledger and payout records: kept for at least 7 years to satisfy tax, accounting, and audit obligations, even after account deletion.
  • Fraud-prevention records (e.g., device identifiers linked to forfeited accounts): kept as long as necessary to prevent repeat abuse.
  • Heartbeat and usage telemetry: kept in identifiable form no longer than 24 months.

8. Age and residency

EarnPanel is for US residents aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that an account belongs to someone under 18, we will close it and delete the associated personal information. If you believe a minor has an account, contact us at the address below.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit, access controls, and signed verification of survey partner callbacks. No system is perfectly secure; please use a strong, unique password for your account email.

10. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and update the date above. For material changes, we will notify you in the app or by email before the changes take effect.

11. Contact
