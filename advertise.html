Pay only for attention you can verify.

EarnPanel is a small always-on-top panel on our users' Macs. Our users are paid a share of what you pay us — which means we can guarantee something no impression-based ad buy can: your message was actually seen, by a real signed-in person, who proved it.

Early beta. First campaigns run to our founding user cohort at pilot pricing that reflects the current audience size — we'd rather under-promise reach and over-deliver proof.

What you can buy

  • Verified engagements (watch & answer). Your video plays in the panel with a visible timer; the reward unlocks only after it finishes and the viewer answers a comprehension question you approve. You pay per verified engagement — completed views with a correct answer. Skipped, abandoned, or failed views cost nothing.
  • Sponsored cards. A clearly-labeled card pinned in the panel's sponsored slot for a flat weekly rate. Impressions are counted only while the panel is actually unobstructed on screen — we bill visible seconds, not "served" impressions.

Why this beats buying impressions

  1. No bots. Every viewer is a signed-in, payout-verified account on exactly one device. Engagement fraud costs us real payout money, so our fraud incentives are aligned with yours.
  2. No background tabs. We track window occlusion at the OS level — if your card is covered by another window, it isn't counted.
  3. Proof, not vibes. Your campaign report is per-event: verified engagements, completion rate, answer accuracy, visible-time distribution. Exportable, auditable.

Content policy (short version)

We'd rather stay small than make the panel feel like a scam feed.
